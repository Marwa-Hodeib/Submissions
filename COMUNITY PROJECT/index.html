Hello and welcome to WTF –
the website that will answer your questions, deal with your concerns,
and give you loads of ideas for making
the absolute best of your teenage years.

WTF is packed with information and advice to help you become happier and feel more in control of your life, in the little things as well as the big important issues. Whatever you want to do – whether it’s boiling an egg or planning your future career – you’ll find sensible, practical, honest advice, written specifically for teenage girls.

Click on the pictures below or use ‘Search’ to find what you’re looking for – search for anything that may be concerning you or anything you’re curious about. If there’s something you can’t find, please let us know! We’re constantly adding and updating information, and are always on the look-out for new ideas to make your life better and happier. Follow us on Instagram and Twitter to keep up to date (links in sidebar), and follow the Agnes blog. We would love Agnes to help as many girls as possible, so if you like our content please share it with your friends. Let’s all help to make everyone’s life better. Thank you!
